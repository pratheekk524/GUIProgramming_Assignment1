Written Reviews

Pat's Peak

This is the mountain I am the most familiar with. I have been going here ever since I was in middle school and it has over 15 trails with various difficulties and terrain. Definitely one of my favorite places to ski.

Cannon Mountain

This is the biggest mountain I have been to with many of the courses being very long and taking me over 20 minutes to complete one trail. Despite this being the mountain I fractured my arm at, I very much enjoyed my experience at this location, though it was unofortuante that it was icy that day I went snowboarding. They also have a very cool waterpark in their resort.

Elk Mountain

I went here for two days to snowboard and I thoroughly enjoyed snowboarding with my friends. The food here was very good as well and the resting cabin was very large and cozy. The food was a little pricy though. Slightly smaller than the formerly mentioned mountains but still had many trails to offer.

Mcintyre Ski Area

I went here because they were doing a deal for $20 tickets, however there was only one small hill to ride down. Personally I don't think it was worth the price because of how small the area was.
