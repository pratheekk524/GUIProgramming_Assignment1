Experiences

My Journey

The Beginning

I first started snowboarding when I was in 6th grade through my school's ski program. A funny story is that I actually wanted to ski originally because a girl I liked at that time was skiing. However when I told my dad I wanted to ski, he picked snowboarding instead saying that snowboarding would be more likely to impress her. Funnily enough, I ended up liking snowboarding more than her. I had originally planned to snowboard alongside her but I ended up making more friends who snowboarded and rode alongside them instead. Ever since my first year of snowboarding, I knew that it was something I wanted to do for the rest of my life. Within my first year, I quickly mastered the basics, so quickly in fact that the instructors moved me up to a higher difficulty class. I had continued going every winter season to continue mastering more challenging concepts in snowboarding until high school.

My Injury

I tried to continue snowboarding through high school, so I signed up for my school's ski club. During one of our ski trips I had numerous small falls on the icy mountain, which all contributed me to fracturing my arm. Back when I first started, my mother was against me snowboarding because she hated me doing dangerous activities. So when I got back from the trip and told her that I hurt my arm she took me to get it checked out and I found out my arm was fractured. When my mom heard what the doctor said, she was very upset that I hurt myself while snowboarding, so much so that I was barred from doing it for many years.

The Return

Once I got to college, I decided to finally get back into snowboarding. My freshman year I went snowboarding for the first time in years with my college friends. I was very rusty when I did it, however within a few hours I started to pick it up again. Riding a snowboard once again made me very excited and I realized that I still enjoyed snowboarding very much. The following year I went to get my own equipment for myself so that I wouldnt have to rent equipment anymore. I spent almost $1500 however I now have my own equipment that I can use, the fact that I had equipment of my own made me very glad, especially since my old ones from middle school are way too small for me now.

Pennsylvania Trip

On February of 2024, my friend Chakshu and I drove down to meet our friend Matthew in Pennsylvania to snowboard together. We spent the weekend in Matthew's home and we snowboarded at a local ski resort. This ski resort was a little smaller than the ski resort that I snowboard at usually, but the view there was amazing. More than anything I enjoyed snowboarding with my friends and it was great to see Matthew since he lives so far away.
